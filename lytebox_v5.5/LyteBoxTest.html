What is LyteBox?

Lytebox is a free, downloadable library and content viewer by Marcus Hay. Check it out at lytebox.com. Using Lytebox, you can easily:
  • Display images and slideshows (think Facebook or Google+ photos)
  • Show information on mouseover
  • Display web content in a nice viewing frame without leaving the page
